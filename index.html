Home

TeleBotz enables remote experts, executives, and caregivers to be present in public places through intelligent telepresence robots. We combine mobility, two-way communication, and real-time control to make remote interaction feel natural and immediate.

Products

Our product family is designed for public-space operations with secure connectivity, intuitive controls, and robust hardware for daily usage.

  • TeleBotz Core: Essential telepresence system for indoor navigation and communication.
  • TeleBotz Assist: Accessibility-focused robot for patient support, guidance, and remote consultation.
  • TeleBotz Pro: Enterprise-grade deployment package with fleet management and analytics.

Usecases

  • Hospitals: Remote specialist rounds, family visits, and care-team coordination.
  • Airports & Transit: Passenger assistance, multilingual support, and incident response.
  • Campuses: Virtual guest lectures, accessibility support, and administrative walkthroughs.
  • Retail & Events: Remote concierge services and VIP guided interactions.

Deployments
